Meet Mike

Your Trusted Real Estate Partner

What sets Mike apart from other real estate agents is the rare combination of skills and experience he brings to the table. In addition to being a Realtor, Mike has over 10 years of experience as a real estate investor and property manager, which has given him a deep understanding of property valuation, market trends, and what it takes to maximize an investment. This hands-on knowledge allows him to offer insights that go beyond typical real estate advice.

Mike also has a proven track record in negotiation and deal-making, developed throughout his previous career leading IT organizations, strategy, and operations for both large corporations and small startups. This background has equipped him with the skills to handle complex transactions, advocate for clients’ best interests, and achieve results that truly work for them.

Whether you’re buying, selling, or investing, Mike’s personalized approach ensures a seamless and stress-free process from start to finish. When you work with Mike, you’re not just getting a realtor—you’re gaining a strategic partner who will go to bat for you every step of the way!
